O-Results Finder

About

Looking for results for your newsletter? Or to see how your clubmates have done at recent events?

O-Results Finder is the quick and easy way to search for your clubmembers' results, straight from the BOF website!

How to use this

  1. Head over to the BOF results page
  2. Navigate (get it?) to the page of the event you want to search on
  3. Paste the url into the form below
  4. Enter the club abbreviation that you want results from
  5. Please also put your name in, this is for analytical purposes.
